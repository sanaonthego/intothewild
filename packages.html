5 Nights / 6 Days

Darjeeling & Kalimpong

From
₹18,500
per person

A perfect blend of colonial charm, misty tea gardens and mountain sunrises. This circuit covers the best of the Darjeeling hills while weaving in the lesser-visited flower market town of Kalimpong.

🌅 Tiger Hill Sunrise 🚂 Toy Train Ride 🍵 Tea Garden Walk 🛕 Monasteries 🌸 Kalimpong Market
Enquire on WhatsApp
  • Day 1
    Arrival — NJP / Bagdogra → Darjeeling
    Transfer from NJP Station or Bagdogra Airport. Scenic mountain drive through tea estates. Check-in, rest and evening stroll on Mall Road.
  • Day 2
    Tiger Hill Sunrise + Darjeeling Sightseeing
    Early morning drive to Tiger Hill for Kanchenjunga sunrise. Visit Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and the Japanese Peace Pagoda.
  • Day 3
    Toy Train Ride + Tea Garden Walk
    Morning toy train joyride (Darjeeling–Ghoom–Darjeeling). Afternoon guided walk through a working tea garden with tea tasting session. Free evening on Mall Road.
  • Day 4
    Darjeeling → Kalimpong
    Morning drive to Kalimpong via Teesta Bridge. Check-in. Visit Deolo Hill viewpoint and the colourful local flower nurseries that supply markets across India.
  • Day 5
    Kalimpong Local Sightseeing
    Visit Zong Dog Palri Fo-Brang Monastery, Mangal Dham Temple, and the busy Kalimpong Haat market. Optional cactus garden and Durpin Dara Hill.
  • Day 6
    Departure
    After breakfast, transfer to NJP Station or Bagdogra Airport. Safe travels!

✓ Included

  • Accommodation (5 nights)
  • Daily breakfast & dinner
  • All transfers by private cab
  • Sightseeing as per itinerary
  • Toy train joyride ticket
  • Tea garden guided walk
  • All toll, parking & driver charges

✗ Not Included

  • Airfare / train tickets
  • Lunch & personal expenses
  • Camera / monument entry fees
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips & gratuities

7 Nights / 8 Days

Offbeat Sikkim

From
₹24,900
per person

Sikkim beyond the tourist bubble — village homestays, high-altitude valleys in bloom, and roads where you'll barely see another traveller. Best visited April–June or Sept–November.

❄️ Yumthang Valley 🏔️ Zero Point 🏡 Village Homestays 🌸 Rhododendron Forest 🛕 Ancient Monasteries
Enquire on WhatsApp
  • Day 1
    Arrival — NJP → Gangtok
    Transfer to Gangtok (125 km). Arrive, check-in and explore MG Marg in the evening. Dinner at a local restaurant.
  • Day 2
    Gangtok Local Sightseeing
    Rumtek Monastery, Enchey Monastery, Flower Exhibition Centre, Do-Drul Chorten Stupa and Tashi Viewpoint.
  • Day 3
    Gangtok → Lachung
    Drive north to Lachung (115 km) through Chungthang. En route see Seven Sisters Waterfall and Naga Falls. Village homestay overnight.
  • Day 4
    Yumthang Valley + Zero Point
    Early morning excursion to Yumthang Valley (3,564 m) — the Valley of Flowers. Optional extension to Zero Point (4,800 m, seasonal). Return to Lachung.
  • Day 5
    Lachung → Offbeat Village Stay
    Drive back south and branch off to an offbeat village — Dzongu, Hee-Bermiok or Tashiding area (depending on season and availability). Village homestay, local dinner.
  • Day 6–7
    Village Life + Offbeat Exploration
    Two full days exploring trails, monasteries and local villages with a local guide. Nature walks, farm experiences, and interactions with Lepcha communities.
  • Day 8
    Departure — NJP / Bagdogra
    Morning transfer back to NJP Station or Bagdogra Airport. Tour ends with memories to last a lifetime.

✓ Included

  • Accommodation (7 nights — mix of hotel & homestay)
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch & dinner)
  • All transfers by private cab
  • Sikkim Protected Area Permit (PAP)
  • Yumthang Valley entry & jeep charges
  • Local village guide (2 days)
  • All tolls, parking & driver charges

✗ Not Included

  • Airfare / train tickets to NJP
  • Zero Point charges (seasonal, optional)
  • Personal expenses & tips
  • Travel insurance
  • Monument / camera fees

3 Nights / 4 Days

Dooars Jungle Safari

From
₹12,500
per person

Deep inside the Terai forests at the foothills of the Himalayas — one-horned rhinos, wild elephants, leopards and over 300 species of birds. Best October to April.

🦏 Rhino Sighting 🐘 Elephant Safari 🌿 Gorumara Forest 🔥 Bonfire Evenings 🐦 Birdwatching
Enquire on WhatsApp
  • Day 1
    Arrival — NJP → Lataguri / Gorumara
    Transfer to the forest edge. Check in to eco-lodge. Evening jungle walk and campfire with a forest naturalist.
  • Day 2
    Gorumara National Park Safari
    Early morning jeep safari into Gorumara — home to one-horned rhino, gaur, leopard, and abundant birdlife. Afternoon watchtower visit at Rhino Point.
  • Day 3
    Jaldapara Elephant Safari + Chilapata
    Drive to Jaldapara Wildlife Sanctuary for an elephant-back safari. Visit the ancient Chilapata Forest fort ruins. Evening bonfire and folk music at camp.
  • Day 4
    Departure
    After breakfast, transfer back to NJP Station or Bagdogra Airport.

✓ Included

  • Eco-lodge accommodation (3 nights)
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch & dinner)
  • Jeep safari in Gorumara (1 session)
  • Elephant safari in Jaldapara
  • Nature guide for forest walks
  • All transfers by private cab

✗ Not Included

  • Airfare / train tickets
  • Alcohol & personal expenses
  • Extra jeep safari rounds
  • Travel insurance
